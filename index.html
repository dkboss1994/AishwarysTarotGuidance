Meet Aishwarya

I was 19 when the cards first chose me. I wasn't looking for them — a worn deck appeared at a flea market, wrapped in violet silk, and something in the universe said: this is yours.

For over six years, I've sat with people at crossroads, in grief, in love, and in confusion. I hold no claim to predict the future — instead, I help you hear what you already know, reflected back through the ancient language of symbols.

Every reading I give is a sacred exchange. I show up with full presence, deep intuition, and an open heart — always.
